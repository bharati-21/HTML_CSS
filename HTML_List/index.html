Paneer Bhurji (Scrambled Cottage Cheese)

Paneer Bhurji is a popular amd delicious North Indian breakfast recipe made from scrambled Indian Cottage Cheese.

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Spices
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes

Method

  1. Crumble paneer (cottage cheese) and keep aside
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Wait for them to crackle
  3. Add chopped onions and saute until they become translucent and soft
  4. Add Ginger-Garlic paste and chopped green chillies
  5. Saute until the raw aroma of ginger-garlic goes away
  6. Add finely chopped tomatoes, and saute until they soften
  7. Once the oil starts releasing, add spices and a pinch of salt
  8. Saute the mixture and cook for a few minutes
  9. Add paneer and cook for a minute
  10. Add coriander leaves and serve it with hot bread or roti
